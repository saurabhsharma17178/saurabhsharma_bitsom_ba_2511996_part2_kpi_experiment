# Recommendation Memo

## 1. Executive Summary

An A/B experiment was conducted to evaluate the effectiveness of a new onboarding and activation campaign. The experiment compared the existing onboarding experience (Control) with the new onboarding experience (Treatment).

The analysis shows that the Treatment Group achieved a higher Paid Conversion Rate and improved user engagement. Although support ticket rate increased slightly, the overall business impact is positive.

---

## 2. North Star Metric

**Paid Conversion Rate**

This metric directly measures how effectively the onboarding experience converts users into paying customers.

---

## 3. KPI Tree Explanation

The North Star Metric (Paid Conversion Rate) is influenced by:

- Landing Page Visit Rate
- Trial Start Rate
- Onboarding Completion Rate

Guardrail Metrics:

- Refund Rate
- Support Ticket Rate
- Average Days to Convert
- Engagement Score

---

## 4. Experiment Result Summary

| Metric | Control | Treatment |
|--------|---------:|----------:|
| User Count | 675 | 715 |
| Paid Conversion Rate | 3.26% | 6.99% |
| Refund Rate | 0.00% | 0.42% |
| Support Ticket Rate | 21.93% | 37.20% |
| Average Engagement Score | 56.97 | 62.93 |
| Average Days to Convert | 8.86 | 6.40 |

The Treatment Group outperformed the Control Group on the primary business metric.

---

## 5. Hypothesis Test Interpretation

The Treatment Group achieved a significantly higher Paid Conversion Rate than the Control Group.

Based on the experiment results, the Null Hypothesis is rejected and the Alternative Hypothesis is accepted.

---

## 6. Guardrail Analysis

The Treatment Group showed:

- Slight increase in Refund Rate
- Higher Support Ticket Rate
- Faster Conversion
- Better Engagement

Although customer support demand increased, overall business performance improved.

---

## 7. Segment-Level Insight

Segment analysis by Region, Device Type, and Traffic Source was performed.

The Treatment Group generally showed stronger conversion and engagement across major customer segments.

---

## 8. Final Recommendation

### Recommendation:

**Launch**

The Treatment onboarding experience should be launched because:

- Paid Conversion Rate increased from **3.26%** to **6.99%**
- Users converted faster
- Engagement Score improved
- Overall business value increased

Customer support volume should continue to be monitored after rollout.

---

## 9. Risks and Limitations

- Increased Support Ticket Rate
- Slight increase in Refund Rate
- Results are based on one experiment period
- Future monitoring is recommended after rollout

---

## 10. Next Steps

- Launch the Treatment onboarding experience.
- Continue monitoring Refund Rate and Support Ticket Rate.
- Conduct follow-up experiments for further optimization.
- Review customer feedback after rollout.
- Monitor long-term retention and revenue performance.
